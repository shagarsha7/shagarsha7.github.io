Who we are?

Wedding Photography and Wedding Film

We prefer a journalistic approach. We are not directors, but reporters, so in a discreet and complete way we reflect the extraordinary atmosphere of your great day and the accompanying emotions. The dates of outdoor sessions are set individually for each couple. We are open to taking pictures on the wedding day as well as on any other of your choice.We have a certificate that allows us to photograph church ceremonies issued by the Diocesan Curia. If you like our approach to work and photos, we will meet and discuss the details of cooperation.

We value the naturalness of all photos and videos taken. We assume that the role of the filmmaker and photographer at the wedding is largely based on being the least visible. We act as observers. Setting guests and the bride and groom asking for a wide smile is simply unnatural, contrary to our principles of work. We try to make you feel at ease with me. We want to show what you really feel in depth. Good wedding photography is a powerful emotional charge combined with spontaneity. If that's what you are looking for, we invite you to cooperation.
